----------------- Creational Patterns ----------------

AbstractFactory: Factory Method, Prototype, Singleton(Concrete Factory is Singleton)
Builder: AbstractFactory, Composite
FactoryMethod: AbstractFactory, TemplateMethod, Prototype
Prototype: AbstractFactory, Composite, Decorator
Singleton: AbstractFactory, Builder, Prototype

----------------- Structural Patterns ----------------

Adapter: Bridge, Decorator, Proxy, FactoryMethod
Bridge: AbstractFactory, Adapter
Composite: ChainOfResponsibility, Decorator, Flyweight, Iterator, Visitor
Decorator: Adapter, Composite, Strategy
Facade: AbstractFactory, Mediator, Singleton
Flyweight: Composite, State, Strategy
Proxy: Adapter, Decorator, Iterator

----------------- Behavioral Patterns ----------------

ChainOfResponsibility: Composite
Command: Composite, Memento, Prototype
Interpreter: Composite, Flyweight, Iterator, Visitor
Iterator: Composite, FactoryMethod, Memento, TemplateMethod
Mediator: Facade, Observer
Memento: Command, Iterator
Observer: Mediator, Singleton
State: Flyweight, Singleton
Strategy: Flyweight
TemplateMethod: FactoryMethod, Strategy
Visitor: Composite, Interpreter
